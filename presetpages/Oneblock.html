Oneblock Preset

by theTWIXhunter

Download Preset
Start on a single block and progressively unlock resources and challenges as you break and expand their world.
  • Includes a spawn island that serves as the central hub for all players in the game. Surrounding this spawn island are multiple unique islands, with each one designated for an individual player. This setup fosters a sense of personalized space while encouraging interaction among players. To access their designated island, players can simply use the command /island, which seamlessly teleports them to their own private area. Each island is crafted with distinct features and resources, allowing players to gather materials, build structures, and complete challenges in solitude or in collaboration with others.
